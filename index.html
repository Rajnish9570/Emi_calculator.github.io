What is EMI ?
EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is a fixed payment to pay at fixed interval for particular time.

Advantages of EMI.

  • Affordability
  • Flexibility
  • Easy on the Wallet
  • Personal Financial Planing

    • Disadvantages of EMI.

  • Long term debt
  • Additional cost
  • Prepayment Penalty
